## To change an event's time or duration

In **Day** and **Week** views, events can be rescheduled directly on the grid — no need to open the edit form.

### Move an event

Press and drag the body of an event up or down to change its **start time**. The event keeps its duration as it moves. In **Week** view, dragging left or right also moves the event to a different **day**.

### Resize an event

Drag the **bottom edge** of an event to change its **end time** — that is, make it longer or shorter. The start time stays put. An event can't be shrunk below 15 minutes.

Both gestures snap to the nearest **15 minutes**, and the event stays within the visible grid. The new time is saved as soon as you release.

## What you can't drag yet

- **Repeating events** can't be dragged. Open the event and edit its time in the form instead — changing a single occurrence of a series isn't supported here yet.
- Events on **subscribed (read-only) calendars** can't be moved or resized.

On a touch device, the same gestures work: drag the event body to move it, or drag its bottom edge to resize.
